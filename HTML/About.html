Our History

Nestled in Raipur, the capital of Chhattisgarh, MAIC today stands as a premier institution proudly managed by the Shree Maharaja Agrasen Charitable Trust. Our Trustees are esteemed dignitaries dedicated to fostering the socio-cultural and economic upliftment of the state. Founded in 2006, MAIC has been at the forefront of providing quality education to the children of our society, making significant strides towards this noble goal.
From its inception, starting with a humble beginning of 96 students in the academic year 2006-2007, now we've nurtured thousands of students who have gone on to make significant contributions in their fields MAIC has proven to be one of the leading institutions in the region, excelling in academics, co-curricular activities, skill development programs, the Rovers and Rangers, MAIC Band, social service initiatives, and sports and physical education.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be an exemplary institution dedicated to fostering talent through value-based education in scientific knowledge and humanities, promoting holistic development for a vibrant and inclusive society.


Mission: To provide lifelong education by instilling patriotic fervor and global competitiveness, enabling faculty, staff, and students to develop the wisdom to uphold family and social values alongside scientific understanding for a harmonious and sustainable society.

Our Achievements

  • Accreditation & Ranking: Accredited with a B+ grade by NAAC.
  • Best Educational College in Chhattisgarh: Awarded by the Asia Education Summit in 2014, with the honor presented by prominent figures.
  • Faculty Excellence: Recognised with the MAIC-PINNACLE (Best Faculty Award) 2023-24.
  • JCI Excellence: JCI Raipur MAIC United achieved multiple awards, including "Outstanding Local Organization of Zone" (Runner Up), "7 Star Trophy," and recognition in "JCI Rise".
  • Community Service: The Rover & Ranger team is active in social welfare, organizing food distribution, "Save the Bird" campaigns, and installing water kiosks.
  • Recognized for "Best in Discipline" at the JCI Super Chapter and individual awards for members in competitions.
